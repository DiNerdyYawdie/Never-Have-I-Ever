Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I add custom statements?

From the main menu, select "Custom Statements," then click the "Add" button. Enter your custom "Never Have I Ever" statement and click "Add" to save it. Free users can create up to 5 custom statements. Premium users have unlimited custom statements.

How do I unlock the Spicy category?

The Spicy category is a premium feature. Click on "Unlock Full Access" from the main menu or try to select the locked Spicy category, and you'll be prompted to purchase the premium unlock for $4.99. This is a one-time purchase with lifetime access.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you've purchased premium features but they're not showing as unlocked:

  1. Open the premium purchase screen
  2. Click "Restore Purchases" at the bottom
  3. Your premium features should be restored automatically

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

Can I edit or delete custom statements?

Yes! Go to "Custom Statements" from the main menu. Each statement has a "Delete" button. Click it to remove a statement. Currently, editing is done by deleting and recreating the statement.

How do I select which categories to play?

From the main menu, select "Categories." You'll see all available categories. Click on each category to toggle it on or off (you'll see a checkmark when selected). Your selections are saved automatically for next time.

What is "Custom Only" mode?

Custom Only mode allows you to play with only your custom statements, ignoring all pre-loaded categories. Enable it from the Categories screen using the toggle at the top.

How do I turn off sound effects?

Go to the Categories screen and you'll find a sound toggle button. Click it to turn sounds on or off.

How do achievements work?

Achievements unlock automatically as you play. View your progress from the main menu by selecting "Achievements." There are 16 achievements to unlock based on gameplay milestones, custom statements created, and special challenges.

What happens when I've seen all the statements?

When you've played through all available statements in your selected categories, a game end screen appears showing your stats. You can play again to see the statements in a new random order, add custom statements, or change your category selection.

Is this app appropriate for children?

The app is rated 12+ on the App Store. Most categories contain age-appropriate content for teens and adults. The Spicy category (premium) contains romantic and suggestive content suitable for ages 18+. Parents should review the content and supervise children's use, especially with custom statements.

Can I get a refund?

Refund requests must be submitted directly to Apple through the App Store. We do not process payments or refunds directly. To request a refund:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your purchase and select "Report a Problem"
  4. Follow Apple's refund request process
Will there be an iPhone or iPad version?

Yes! iPhone and iPad versions are coming soon with universal purchase support. When available, if you've purchased premium on Apple TV, you'll get it free on iPhone/iPad (and vice versa).

Do my custom statements sync across devices?

Currently, custom statements are stored locally on your Apple TV. Cloud sync is a planned premium feature for future updates and will allow syncing across all your devices.

Troubleshooting

App is crashing or freezing

  • Force quit the app and restart it
  • Restart your Apple TV
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Ensure your Apple TV is running the latest tvOS version

Purchase not working

  • Ensure you're signed in to the App Store with your Apple ID
  • Check that in-app purchases are not restricted in Screen Time settings
  • Try the "Restore Purchases" button
  • Contact Apple Support if payment issues persist

Navigation issues with Apple TV remote

  • Make sure your remote is charged and connected
  • Try swiping to navigate between elements
  • Click to select focused items
  • Use the menu button to go back
