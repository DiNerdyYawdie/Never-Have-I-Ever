Privacy Policy

Never Have I Ever for Apple TV

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Never Have I Ever. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Apple TV application.

Information We Collect

We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

What Data Stays on Your Device:

  • Custom Statements: Any custom "Never Have I Ever" statements you create are stored locally on your Apple TV using iOS UserDefaults
  • Achievement Progress: Your unlocked achievements and progress are stored locally
  • Game Statistics: Your total games played, statements viewed, and category preferences are stored locally
  • Category Preferences: Your selected categories are stored locally
  • Settings: Sound preferences and other app settings are stored locally

Data Storage

All data generated by your use of Never Have I Ever is stored exclusively on your Apple TV device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

In-App Purchases

Never Have I Ever offers in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store. When you make a purchase:

  • The transaction is handled entirely by Apple
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • We do not have access to your purchase history beyond what Apple provides for purchase verification
  • Purchase receipts are verified through Apple's servers to confirm your entitlement
  • Your purchase status (whether you own premium features) is stored locally on your device

Third-Party Services

Never Have I Ever does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third-party service we interact with is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchase processing.

Children's Privacy

Never Have I Ever is rated 12+ on the App Store. The app is designed for users aged 12 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. All data remains on the user's device.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any personal information, the security of your data is protected by your device's security measures and Apple's iOS security framework.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to delete, modify, or share. You maintain full control over your data:

  • You can delete custom statements within the app
  • You can reset your achievements and statistics within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@neverhaveiever.app
  • GitHub: github.com/DiNerdyYawdie/Never-Have-I-Ever

Summary

In simple terms: Never Have I Ever is a privacy-first application. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Everything stays on your device. You're in complete control of your data.
